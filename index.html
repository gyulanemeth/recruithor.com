How does it work?

We replace boring and inefficient recruitment processes with a new, challenging and enjoyable process. We select around the top 8% of the developers throughout our testing process, and we connect these developers with our pool of partner companies.

Currently, we only hire beginner, junior, and medior frontend developers who work remotely for our partner companies, and we are focusing on Javascript, Vue.js & React.js. We will soon extend this list with other frontend and also backend technologies.
