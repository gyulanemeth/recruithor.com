How it works

We continuously hire the best frontend developers for our partners.

In a highly unusual process (that is loved by devs) we test the candidates' problem solving skills, their tech knowledge, and finally their algorithmic thinking.

Currently, we only test for the following frontend technologies: Javascript & Vue.js.

We don't hire senior developers for you, because our testing process is only great to test beginner, junior and medior developers.

We only hire for remote companies or teams. Let us know your preferred time-zones.
