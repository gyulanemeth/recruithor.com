How it works

We continuously hire the best frontend developers for our partners.

We take a new approach to an old process reducing initial screening from 1 month to 1 week and picking out the top 8% of the applicants, and we connect these developers with our pool of partner companies.

Currently, we only hire beginner, junior, and medior web developers who work remotely for our partner companies.

We do not test for specific tech stacks at the moment. We believe that a good developer can learn any tech stack in a reasonable amount of time.

  1. 1. Problem solving test

    This is a go/no-go deep-water test. Candidates have to figure out a brain-teaser with almost no input from our side. If a candidate passes this test, it means they are generally a good problem solver, and they can figure out things on their own.

  2. 2. Javascript programming quiz

    In web development, Javascript is unavoidable, so we want to make sure candidates have a good understanding of the language.

  3. 3. Algorithmic thinking interview

    Everybody want to hire developers who can think. Algorithmic skills are a good indicator of this, probably the best one. That is why we test this very thoroughly in a live interview, where candidates have to solve problems on the fly. If someone passes this test, it means they can think through relatively complex things, and they can do it under pressure.

    This test is also a great way to test our candidates' communication skills, as they have to explain their thought process.
